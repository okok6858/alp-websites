About

Independent consulting with a practical edge.

We work alongside executives and boards on the questions that matter: where to compete, how to organise, and what to prioritise when resources and attention are finite.

Our work is grounded in evidence and dialogue—structured workshops, focused analysis, and recommendations you can act on. No boilerplate decks: outcomes you can use in the next quarter and the next chapter.

Services

What we help you deliver.

  • Strategy & portfolio

    Market and competitive clarity, growth options, and a coherent roadmap from ambition to milestones.

  • Operating model

    Roles, decision rights, and ways of working so strategy translates into day-to-day execution.

  • Performance & change

    Programme design, governance, and stakeholder alignment for initiatives that stick.

  • Commercial diligence

    Structured assessments for investments, partnerships, and major commitments—rigorous and proportionate.

  • Leadership facilitation

    Offsites and executive sessions with clear agendas, candid conversation, and documented outcomes.

  • Sector perspective

    Experience across professional services, industrials, and owner-led businesses—applied with judgment, not templates.

Approach

How we work with you.

We start from your context: constraints, culture, and calendar. Engagements are scoped for decision quality—right-sized teams, direct partner involvement, and explicit handoffs to your organisation.

We favour transparency on methods and assumptions, so leaders can explain the “why” to their own teams. When the work is done, you own the narrative—not a dependency on us.
